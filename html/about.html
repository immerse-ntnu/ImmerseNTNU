About Immerse NTNU

What Immerse does

Creates the game developers of tomorrow

In Immerse we learn by doing. You will learn how to make games, and come together as a team to create games. Our goal is to prepare you for your professional life as a programmer.

Who Immerse is for

Enthusiasts with some experience

At Immerse NTNU we are looking for people who are passionate about video games, want to learn about how programming of games works, how to make games and how to work on team-projects.

You will need to have at an intermediate understanding of object-oriented programming. If you have had the courses "Objektorientert programmering" or "Prosedyre- og Objektorientert programmering", that is enough.

What Immerse offers

What you can expect from enrolling at immerse

Immerse offers a community of developers who have the same goal as you: To become a better developer. We also have meetings twice a week to learn or work on projects, and an online community on Discord and Facebook.

  • First semester

    Noob

    In your first semester you will acquire the skills to start making games. You will learn about the Unity game engine, the C# programming language and how to work as a team using github. You will also create some smaller games.

  • Second semester

    Junior developer

    Starting your second semester you become a junior developer and will work on a larger game with some supervision. You will also help members in their first semester.

  • Third semester

    Senior developer

    When you no longer need supervision you will become a senior, aka someone who supervises junior developers.

How you can enroll at Immerse

If you want to join Immerse, simply fill out this form. You can also send the organization a text on Facebook.
